Integrations allow you to connect Ledgerbeam with your favorite tools and platforms, enabling you to export data insights, automate workflows, and access your data from anywhere you work.

## Available Integrations

Ledgerbeam supports integrations with popular platforms including:

### Communication & Collaboration

- **[Slack](/integrations/slack)** - Query data by mentioning @ledgerbeam in your channels
- **[Notion](/integrations/notion)** - Export data insights and reports to your Notion pages
- **[Google Docs](/integrations/google-docs)** - Export data and reports to Google Docs documents

### Analytics & Business Intelligence

- **[Metabase](/integrations/metabase)** - Export data insights and analytics
- **[Retool](/integrations/retool)** - Export data to your Retool applications and internal tools

### Automation & Workflows

- **[Zapier](/integrations/zapier)** - Export data through automated workflows to thousands of apps

### Design & Visualization

- **[Figma](/integrations/figma)** - Export data visualizations and charts to Figma designs
- **[Lucidcharts](/integrations/lucidcharts)** - Export data to create diagrams and charts in Lucidcharts

## Getting Started

Each integration has its own setup process. Browse the integration guides below to find step-by-step instructions for connecting your preferred tools.

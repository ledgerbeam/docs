Connect Ledgerbeam to Notion to export data insights and reports directly to your Notion pages, keeping your team informed with up-to-date analytics.

## Features

- Export query results to Notion pages
- Create formatted reports in Notion
- Sync data automatically to Notion databases
- Share insights with your team through Notion

## Setup Instructions

1. Navigate to the Integrations page in your Ledgerbeam dashboard
2. Click on the Notion integration card
3. Click "Connect to Notion" to authorize Ledgerbeam
4. Select the Notion workspace you want to connect
5. Grant Ledgerbeam access to the pages you want to use
6. Click "Allow" to complete the connection

## Usage

Once connected, you can export data to Notion:

1. Run a query in Ledgerbeam
2. Click the "Export to Notion" option
3. Select the target Notion page or database
4. Choose the export format (table, report, etc.)
5. Your data will be added to Notion

## Export Formats

- **Tables**: Export query results as Notion tables
- **Reports**: Create formatted reports with charts and insights
- **Databases**: Sync data to Notion databases for further analysis
- **Pages**: Create new pages with query results and visualizations

## Permissions

The Notion integration requires:
- Read access to pages you want to export to
- Write access to create or update content
- Access to databases if you're syncing data

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues:

- Verify your Notion workspace connection is active
- Check that Ledgerbeam has the necessary page permissions
- Ensure the target Notion page exists and is accessible
- Review the export logs in your Ledgerbeam dashboard
